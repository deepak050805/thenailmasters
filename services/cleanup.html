Good to Know

Frequently Asked

What's the difference between a cleanup and a facial?

A cleanup focuses on deep cleansing, exfoliation and de-tan without the extended massage and mask stages of a full facial.

How often should I get a cleanup?

Every 3–4 weeks is ideal to keep skin fresh, especially before events.

Is bleach safe for sensitive skin?

We use dermatologically tested bleach and always do a patch check for new clients with sensitive skin.

Can I combine D-Tan with a cleanup?

Yes, D-Tan can be added to any cleanup tier for extra brightening.
