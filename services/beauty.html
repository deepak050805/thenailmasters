Good to Know

Frequently Asked

Honey wax or rica wax — which should I choose?

Rica wax is a premium, gentler formulation suited to sensitive skin and areas like the bikini line, while honey wax is a reliable everyday option for arms and legs.

Is dermaplaning suitable for all skin types?

Dermaplaning works well for most skin types; our estheticians will check your skin during consultation to confirm it's right for you.

How far in advance should I book before an event?

We recommend booking waxing or threading 1–2 days before an event to allow any redness to settle.

Do you use the same wax pot for every client?

No — we follow hospital-grade hygiene practices with fresh wax and single-use applicators for every client.
