Our Story

Founded on precision.
Built on care.

The Nail Masters began with a simple belief — that a manicure should feel like an occasion, not an errand. Every artist on our team trains continuously in extension technique, colour theory and skin-safe practice, so what you leave with looks considered, not templated.

Today, our Sector 67 studio is where Mohali comes for bridal nails, hydra facials and the kind of lash and beauty detailing that holds up to a camera and a calendar full of events.

Good to Know

Frequently Asked

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

Yes — we're an appointment-only studio to keep each session unrushed. We recommend booking at least 1–2 days ahead, and a week ahead for bridal or festive dates.

How long do nail extensions typically last?

With proper care, our acrylic and builder-gel extensions hold for 3–4 weeks. We recommend an infill at the 3-week mark to keep the structure clean.

Do you offer bridal trials?

Yes, we offer a full bridal trial session so you can finalise design, length and shade well ahead of your event day.

What hygiene practices do you follow?

All tools are sanitised between every client using hospital-grade sterilisation, and single-use files/buffers are discarded after each session.

Do you have gift cards or membership plans?

Yes — see our Pricing section above for current membership plans, and message us on WhatsApp for gift card denominations.
